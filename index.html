FNATIC VALORANT HISTORY

Fnatic entered Valorant in 2020 and quickly established themselves as one of the top teams in the EMEA region and the world. Known for their strategic gameplay and individual skill, Fnatic's Valorant team has consistently been a contender in international tournaments.

With a focus on developing young talent and innovative strategies, Fnatic has created a legacy in the Valorant competitive scene, becoming the first team to win a Masters tournament without dropping a single map.
